Poha

Ingredients:

  • Poha or Flattened Rice
  • Onion
  • Green Chillies
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Cumin Seads
  • Mustard Seads
  • Lemon

Steps to make Poha:

  1. Add Poha to a collander and rinse with water.
  2. Strain the water.
  3. Chop onion, green chillies.
  4. Heat a pan with oil and saute some peanuts.
  5. Add cumin and mustard seeds.
  6. Add chopped onion and green chillies.
  7. Add turmeric.
  8. Add poha and mix well.
  9. Cover and steam.
  10. Take off the heat, add salt as per taste and squeez lemon.
